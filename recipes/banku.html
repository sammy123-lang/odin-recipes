Easiest way to prepare banku.

Banku is a local Ghanaian dish made from fermented corn and cassava dough. It is a staple food enjoyed by many Ghanians,due to its ability to be eaten with different soups and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fermented corn dough
  • 1 cup of fermented cassava dough
  • Water
  • salt (optional)

Steps;

  1. Mix both dough with water in your cooking pot until a uniform is formed.
  2. Add considerable amout of salt if prefared.
  3. Stir on heat source of heat until preferable texture.

    4. And your banku is ready to go.Serve with preferable soup or sauce.
