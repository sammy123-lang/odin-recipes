Easiest way to cook rice

Rice is a local Chineese dish introduced to Ghanians aroud the year 1983 when the Ghana faced hunger.

The meal is easy and fast to cook and does not require any special skill to prepare,but will satisfy you.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour adequate amout of rice in a bowl and wash with clean water.
  2. Pour the water out of the bowl leaving the grains of rice behind.
  3. Turn on your heat source e.g your stove or cooker or coal pot and put on moderate heat.
  4. Put your cooking pot on fire and pour in an amout of water more than the amout of your rice.
  5. Add some salt and allow the water to boil.
  6. When the water has boiled for ssome time, pour the rice into the pot.
  7. Fetch excess water until the water is on the same level as the rice.
  8. Allow the rice to cook,but do not throw the water you fetched away as you may need it.
  9. After the water in the pot is drained taste the rice if the salt is enough and the rice has the right texture.
  10. If the salt is not enough for you,fetch a an amout of the water you fetched,with aladdle and add a small amout of salt.
  11. Then sprinkle it on the the rice and cook at low heat.
  12. And if the rice is a little hard,fetch an adequate amout of the water and sprinkle on the rice and cook on low heat.
  13. if you have done step 11 there is no need for step 12.
And there your rice is ready
